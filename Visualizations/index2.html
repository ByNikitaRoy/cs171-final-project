WHAT IS GOING ON?

On February 24, 2022 Russia invaded Ukraine starting the biggest military mobilization in Europe since World War II. The invasion is likely to have major effects on people around the world, and every country's media has been watching.

What can we learn from watching the watchers?

Decades of research have demonstrated that events covered by the media are selected, framed, and shaped by what are called "news values": values that confirm existing images and ideologies.

This project makes an effort to show what the major international news organizations are focusing their attention on using the Ukraine - Russia conflict as a case study.

ABOUT THE PROJECT

THE GDELT DATABASE

The Global Database of Events, Language and Tone is a big data project led by Kalev Leetaru. It collects news reports about events around the world and parses the news reports for actors, events, and geography with the aim of providing a comprehensive set of data for researchers, governments, and civic society.
